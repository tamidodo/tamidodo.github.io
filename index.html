Projects

UBC Co-op: Observations of Supernova Remnant CTA1

Analyzed observational data to better understand the characteristics of the supernova remnant CTA1.

UBC Honours Undergraduate Thesis: Impact of Yarkovsky Effect Modification Due to 2029 Fly-by on the Close Approach Structure of 99942 Apophis

Investigated how the 2029 close approach of Apophis might alter its trajectory through Yarkovsky effect perturbations.

UWO Masters Thesis: Characterizing Meteoroid Origins by Simulating Their Orbital Histories

Developed simulations to trace the origins of meteoroids and understand their contribution to planetary science.

UWO Collaboration with DRDC: Luciole Satellite Tracking

Collaborated with DRDC to improve tracking and analysis methods for the Luciole satellite system.
